Bridging the Skill Gap for a Better Future.

Engaging people in skill-based training and giving them the best possible chance to compete for a job is what we do at SZABIST ZABTech (iTVE). Among the TVET providers shaping the future of Pakistan, we lead the way with our scheme, based upon the students who are about to or have already entered the job market. We prioritize skills mismatches and provide over 100 courses, from entry-level certificates to professional diplomas, thereby giving individuals the necessary expertise for the job market. Despite having over 80,000 trained students for more than 25 years, we make use of different initiatives to achieve our goals such as employment-related training, women empowerment programs, and industry partnerships. Through investments in the skills sector, our goal is to transform lives, boost industries, and consequently strengthen the economy. Join the SZABIST ZABTech family and enjoy the virtues of leaping into a brighter future!

Information Technology (IT)

The IT industry is on the leading edge of the digital revolution with numerous job opportunities in software development, networking, cybersecurity, and the like. ZABTech’s IT courses get students ready for real-world careers in such a completely ever-changing industry.

Manufacturing

Manufacturing is the foundation of industrial growth, encompassing fields such as automation, machine operation, and quality control. ZABTech’s teaching programs assist students to gain the technical know-how needed to operate the latest manufacturing equipment properly.

Construction

The real estate sector is a necessary part of infrastructure development; for instance, people become civil engineers, site managers, and skilled workers. ZABTech’s courses deliver practical know-how by offering real-life projects that will help develop good grounding in the field.

Services

The services sector is made of a wide range of jobs that are part of hospitality, healthcare, retail, and customer service. ZABTech's TVET programs give room for the honing of practical skills, as well as train the students for success in service-oriented careers.
